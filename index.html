What I do

Fractional Operations

Senior operational leadership on a part-time basis — so you get the expertise without the full-time cost. I plug in as the person who owns the messy, cross-functional stuff: keeping vendors accountable, managing critical deadlines, running board and executive operations, and making sure nothing falls through the cracks while your team focuses on growth.

Software Implementation

End-to-end rollout of the platforms that run your business — contract management, board portals, entity management, equity, project management, and productivity tools. I've implemented across dozens of platforms and know how to get teams actually using them, not just licensed for them.

AI & Process Automation

Practical AI workflow design — identifying where automation actually reduces friction, then building or directing the build. Grounded in how legal, business, and ops teams actually work, not in what sounds impressive on a pitch deck.

Compliance & Regulatory Ops

DDQ and KYC program infrastructure, SEC filing support, multi-jurisdiction entity and licensing compliance. Particularly strong in fintech and crypto contexts.

Organizational Systems & Playbooks

If your team tracks renewals, deadlines, and critical dates across a patchwork of spreadsheets and sticky notes, that's a risk — and it's fixable. I build the tickler systems, automated alerts, Slack-integrated reminders, and operational playbooks that put the right information in front of the right person at the right time, automatically. No more chasing. No more surprises. And no more critical institutional knowledge living in one person's head, their local Drive, or a spreadsheet no one else can find.
